About Utah Agile

We unite Utah's agile community, and beyond, by facilitating networking, collaboration, and mentoring for aspiring and experienced professionals.

Utah Agile is a 501(c)6 nonprofit. Membership is free and open to everyone, though we're centered in Utah. Participants earn Scrum Educational Units (SEUs) through the Scrum Alliance — one SEU per hour of involvement in Utah Agile programs.

The Agile Manifesto was born out of the peaks of Snowbird, and Utah continues to be a growing hub for businesses of every size embracing lean and agile methodologies.
