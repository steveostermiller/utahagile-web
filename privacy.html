Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Privacy Notice

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Utah Agile. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this website. It will notify you of the following:

  1. What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the website, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
  2. What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
  3. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.

We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request (e.g. sending information you requested).

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about events, opportunities, or changes to this privacy policy.

Your Access To & Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the email address given on our website:

  • See what data we have about you, if any.
  • Change/correct any data we have about you.
  • Have us delete any data we have about you.
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email at privacy@utahagile.org.

GDPR Compliance Information

Who we are

Our website address is: https://utahagile.org

We are a nonprofit, volunteer-led, professional association.

What personal data we collect and why we collect it

At this time, our website does not have an active email signup or other data collection form. We plan to add a voluntary email newsletter signup in the future (through our email provider, MailerLite); when that feature goes live, this policy will be updated to describe exactly what is collected and how it is used.

Until then, we do not automatically collect any personal information through this website. The only way we receive your information is if you contact us directly, such as by email.

Cookies

Resource: All About Cookies

Our website is a static site and does not set any tracking cookies. We do not currently use any analytics or advertising cookies on this site. If we add features that require cookies (such as a newsletter signup) in the future, we will update this policy and provide a consent option for any non-essential cookies.

Embedded content from other websites

Pages on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.), such as YouTube or Vimeo videos. Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

Those websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracing your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

We do not currently use website analytics.

Who we share your data with

We do not currently collect any personal data through this website, so there is nothing to share. Should that change, we will not sell or rent your information, and will only share it as necessary to fulfill a request you've made.

How long we retain your data

Not applicable at this time, since we do not currently collect data through this website.

Where we send your data

Not applicable at this time, since we do not currently collect data through this website.

Our contact information

How we protect your data

Our website itself is a static site with no server-side database, hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not currently store any personal data collected through the website.

What data breach procedures we have in place

What third parties we receive data from

What automated decision making and/or profiling we do with user data

Not applicable at this time, since we do not currently collect data through this website.
