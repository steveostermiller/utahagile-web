Information collection, use, and sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only collect information that you voluntarily give us via our subscribe form, email, or other direct contact. We will not sell or rent your information, and we only share data as necessary to fulfill your requests. We may contact subscribers about events and opportunities unless you opt out.

Your rights

You can request to see, correct, or delete your data, or raise concerns about how it's used, by contacting privacy@utahagile.org.

What we collect

Our subscribe form requests only your email address, which is added to our newsletter list (managed by MailerLite) for community updates.

Cookies

This site is a static website and does not set tracking cookies by default. If we add analytics in the future, we'll update this policy and provide a consent option for any non-essential cookies.

Data retention & protection

Subscriber emails are retained until you unsubscribe. Data is stored with our newsletter provider (MailerLite), which maintains GDPR-compliant practices. We will notify users of any potential breach affecting their data.
