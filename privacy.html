Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Privacy Notice

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Utah Agile. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this website. It will notify you of the following:

  1. What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the website, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
  2. What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
  3. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.

We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request (e.g. sending information you requested).

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about events, opportunities, or changes to this privacy policy.

Your Access To & Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the email address given on our website:

  • See what data we have about you, if any.
  • Change/correct any data we have about you.
  • Have us delete any data we have about you.
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email at privacy@utahagile.org.

GDPR Compliance Information

Who we are

Our website address is: https://utahagile.org

We are a nonprofit, volunteer-led, professional association.

What personal data we collect and why we collect it

Subscribe Form

Our website contains a voluntary Subscribe Form. The single data input field requests an email.

When a site visitor enters an email into our Subscribe Form, that email is automatically added to our mailing list with MailerLite, our email newsletter provider. This is a distribution list for emails containing community updates.

Cookies

Resource: All About Cookies

Our website is a static site and does not itself set tracking cookies. Our Subscribe Form is provided by MailerLite, which may set its own cookies when you interact with it — see MailerLite's privacy policy for details.

We do not currently use any analytics or advertising cookies on this site. If that changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide a consent option for any non-essential cookies.

Embedded content from other websites

Pages on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.), such as YouTube or Vimeo videos. Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

Those websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracing your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

We do not currently use website analytics.

Who we share your data with

We don't share any data with anyone. The email a visitor submits through our Subscribe Form on our website will only be used to send you information about upcoming events or other information we think will be interesting to you as a member of this community.

How long we retain your data

If you subscribe, your email is retained indefinitely. If you unsubscribe via clicking that option in a Utah Agile email communication, your email is tagged not to receive further communication.

Where we send your data

We do not send subscriber list information anywhere beyond our email newsletter provider, MailerLite.

Our contact information

Contact us for any questions at privacy@utahagile.org

How we protect your data

Subscriber emails are stored with MailerLite, our email newsletter provider. Our website itself is a static site with no server-side database, hosted on GitHub Pages.

What data breach procedures we have in place

We notify all involved when we receive any notice of possible breach from our email newsletter provider or website host.

What third parties we receive data from

We don't receive any data from third parties.

What automated decision making and/or profiling we do with user data

When someone enters an email into our Subscribe Form, an automatic, welcome communication is sent to the email entered. The welcome email communication includes a link to unsubscribe.
