Earn SEUs

Participate in Utah Agile programs and earn Scrum Educational Units through the Scrum Alliance.

Members earn one SEU per hour of involvement in Utah Agile programs — meetups, roundtables, and conferences all count toward maintaining your Scrum Alliance certifications.

How to earn

  1. Attend a Utah Agile event (see upcoming events).
  2. Claim one SEU per hour of participation in your Scrum Alliance dashboard under "Category C: Volunteering / Events."

Questions about claiming SEUs? Email info@agileutah.org.
