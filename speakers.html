Become a speaker

Have an agile topic or trend you'd like to share? Let us know.

Utah Agile invites community members to share their expertise on agile topics and current trends. Before proposing, take a look at our past speakers and topics on the Events page.

How to propose a talk

Send your proposal to info@utahagile.org with:

  • A short background on your agile experience
  • Your presentation title
  • A 250-word (or less) summary of your idea
  • A contact phone number and email
